FAQ • తరచుగా అడిగే ప్రశ్నలు

Your Questions,
Answered

Everything you need to know before going solar.

How much does a solar system cost?
సోలార్ సిస్టమ్ ఖర్చు ఎంత?
A 1 kW system costs approximately ₹60,000–70,000 before subsidy. With government subsidy of up to ₹78,000, a 3 kW system can cost as low as ₹1.2 lakhs net after deduction.
How long does installation take?
ఇన్‌స్టాలేషన్‌కి ఎంత సమయం పడుతుంది?
Typical residential installation is completed within 1–2 working days. DISCOM commissioning and net meter installation takes 7–15 days after installation.
What happens during a power cut?
కరెంట్ పోతే ఏమవుతుంది?
Standard on-grid systems shut down during power cuts for safety. With a battery backup system or hybrid inverter, you can continue running your home even during grid failures.
Is my roof suitable for solar?
నా పైకప్పు సోలార్‌కి అనుకూలంగా ఉంటుందా?
Most RCC, tile, and sheet roofs are suitable. A south-facing roof is ideal. We conduct a free site survey to evaluate your specific roof and give recommendations.
What is the lifespan of solar panels?
సోలార్ ప్యానెల్స్ ఎంత కాలం పనిచేస్తాయి?
Quality solar panels have a performance warranty of 25 years and a product warranty of 10–12 years. Most panels continue generating power for 30+ years with minimal degradation.
How does net metering work?
నెట్ మీటరింగ్ ఎలా పని చేస్తుంది?
Excess solar energy you generate is exported to the grid and credited to your account at the feed-in tariff rate. Your monthly bill deducts these credits from consumption charges.
Can I get a loan for solar installation?
సోలార్ కోసం లోన్ తీసుకోవచ్చా?
Yes! Solar loans are available from SBI, Canara Bank, and other nationalized banks at 7% interest under PM Surya Ghar scheme. We help you with the loan application process.
Do panels work in cloudy weather?
మేఘావృతమైన వాతావరణంలో పని చేస్తాయా?
Yes, modern solar panels generate electricity even on cloudy days — typically at 10–25% efficiency. South India's climate is excellent for solar with 300+ sunny days per year on average.
